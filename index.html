Wood Gathering

Rules

Gathering wood during your downtime allows for you to hit a tree a possible 20 times

Hit the tree with absolute precision. A D6 is then rolled. Anything but a 1, returns wood equal to the dice rolled. There is a chance at receiving special resources during a Precision hit. Rolling a 1 on the D6 will give the player one stack on their Fail Count.

Hit the tree as hard as possible! A D20 is then rolled. Any roll that is 7 or over returns wood equal to the dice rolled. There is NO chance at receiving special resources during a Hard Hit. Rolling a 6 or lower on the D20 results in the player receiving one stack to their Fail Count
